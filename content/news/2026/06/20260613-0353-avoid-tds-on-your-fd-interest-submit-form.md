## Who is affected

Fixed deposit holders earning significant interest income.

## What changes

Banks will deduct 10% TDS if interest exceeds ₹50,000 (or ₹1 lakh for seniors) unless Form 121 is submitted.

## Why this matters

Proactive tax management prevents unnecessary TDS deductions.

## What you can do

Check your annual interest income and submit Form 121 to your bank if eligible.

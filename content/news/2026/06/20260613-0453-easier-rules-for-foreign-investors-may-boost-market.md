## Who is affected

All equity investors as simplified norms could attract more global capital into Indian markets.

## What changes

SEBI is planning to simplify KYC and disclosure norms for foreign portfolio investors (FPIs).

## Why this matters

Increased ease of doing business for global funds generally supports market depth and stability.

## What you can do

Continue your SIPs as usual; focus on long-term wealth creation rather than reacting to institutional flow changes.

## Who is affected

Salaried taxpayers and business owners with tax liability exceeding ₹10,000.

## What changes

The first installment of advance tax for FY 2026-27 is due by June 15.

## What you can do

Calculate your estimated annual tax liability and pay the first installment to avoid interest penalties.

## Who is affected

Salaried taxpayers and business owners who were defaulted into the new tax regime.

## What changes

You retain the legal right to opt for the old tax regime during ITR filing, regardless of your employer's default selection.

## What you can do

Calculate your total tax liability under both regimes before filing your ITR to ensure you claim all eligible deductions.

## Who is affected

Investors dealing in crypto assets must now maintain precise transaction records for tax filings.

## What changes

New reporting requirements under Schedule VDA require detailed transaction entries cross-referenced with exchange data.

## Why this matters

Increased scrutiny and compliance requirements add administrative burden for retail crypto investors.

## What you can do

Download your transaction history from all exchanges and organize your records early to avoid penalties.

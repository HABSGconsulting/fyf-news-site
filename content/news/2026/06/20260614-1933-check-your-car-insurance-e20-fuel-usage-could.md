## Who is affected

Car owners with older vehicles should verify fuel compatibility to avoid insurance claim rejection.

## What changes

Using E20 fuel in non-compatible engines may be classified as negligence by insurers.

## What you can do

Check your vehicle manual or contact your manufacturer to confirm if your car is E20 compatible.

## Who is affected

Salaried taxpayers preparing to file their annual income tax returns.

## What changes

Form 16 serves as the primary document for reporting total income and tax deducted at source (TDS).

## What you can do

Cross-verify the income, TDS, and deductions mentioned in your Form 16 against your bank statements and investment proofs.

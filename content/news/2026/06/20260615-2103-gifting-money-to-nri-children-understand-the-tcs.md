## Who is affected

Salaried taxpayers and business owners sending money abroad.

## What changes

Remittances under LRS attract TCS, though gifts to children are tax-free in their hands.

## What you can do

Consult your tax advisor to calculate the exact TCS impact before initiating large foreign transfers.

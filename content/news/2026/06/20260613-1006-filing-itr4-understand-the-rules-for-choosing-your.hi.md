किसे असर होगा

Business owners aur professionals jo ITR-4 (Sugam) file karte hain.

## क्या बदलेगा

Business income ke liye old aur new tax regime ke beech switch karne ke liye niyam hain.

## यह क्यों ज़रूरी है

Tax compliance ke liye sahi regime chunna zaroori hai.

## आप क्या कर सकते हैं

ITR file karne se pehle apni business income aur tax regime ka sahi se vishleshan karein.
